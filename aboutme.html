Hi! I am Diego,

Profile

During the years I studied architecture at the university not only did I learn about architecture, but I learned about how to face and solve problems properly.
As I see it, the discipline of architecture is made up of numerous subjects, being the architect's job to know a little of everyone of them; and thus be able fo find the more suitable solution. Space design, composition of architectonic elements, construction details, and the inevitable relations between them, were the subjects which strongly defined my interest and university practice.

Regarding my work experience, beginning as an intern at such a large project as Sagrada Familia implicates that the drawing of blueprints and documentation, along with its tridimensional modelling, has to be coordinated and developed simultaniously by few departments. This has helped me reinforce my teamwork skills and has challenged me to adapt to unexpected changes, while being more receptive to new ideas and reviews. Furthermore, being able to work on an ongoing building, the site visits also allowed me to see on firsthand how blueprints and solutions are directly translated to construction.

Currently, my personal curiosity has focused my attention onto exploring other disciplines, such as photography or programming, where I keep practicing some of the same subjects and increasing my knowledge with new ones. This Web Portfolio is the first outcome.
